Introduction

BNCET Alumni Committee

BNGI is deeply committed to maintaining and strengthening ties with its alumni community, emphasizing that these relationships are invaluable. The Alumni Connect initiative remains a cornerstone of BNGIâ€™s efforts, reflecting our belief in the enduring impact and importance of our alumni network
We take immense pride in our extensive network of over 12500 alumni who have established themselves as leaders in various industries worldwide. Their invaluable insights and guidance, shared during campus visits, empower students with the latest industry trends and job market knowledge. This dynamic interaction not only enriches our students learning experience but also enhances our placement efforts, often resulting in numerous pre-placements offers even before the completion of their courses at BN Group of Institution.

Objectives

We are driven by a set of core objectives that revolve around three key pillars:

Contribution

Foster lifelong relationships: We nurture the bonds forged during your time at BNCET by providing opportunities for alumni to reconnect, reminisce, and build bridges across generations.
Create a network of support: We stand as a source of mutual assistance and mentorship, offering career guidance, professional development opportunities, and personal support to our members.
Enhance student-alumni engagement: We facilitate interaction between alumni and current students, bridging the gap between past and present, and sharing invaluable experiences and insights.

Connection

Celebrate and sustain our alma mater: We actively contribute to the growth and success of BNCET by advocating for its mission, supporting fundraising initiatives, and sharing our expertise in various fields.
Promote academic excellence: We provide scholarships, awards, and mentorship programs to empower current students and enhance academic opportunities. Contribute to the wider community: We leverage the diverse talents and resources of our members to engage in meaningful social and civic initiatives, creating a positive impact beyond the walls of our institution.

Legacy

Preserve the unique spirit of BNCET: We document and celebrate the shared history of our alma mater, ensuring its traditions and values live on for future generations.
Embrace continuous improvement: We strive to evolve and adapt, responding to the changing needs of our members and the institution, constantly seeking new ways to connect, contribute, and create a lasting legacy.
